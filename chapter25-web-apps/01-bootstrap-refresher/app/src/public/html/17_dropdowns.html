Dropdowns A toggleable, contextual overlay for displaying lists of items.

Dropdowns are toggleable, contextual overlays for displaying lists of links and more. They're made interactive with JavaScript.
They are toggled by clicking, not by hovering.

Note: This component requires JavaScript
